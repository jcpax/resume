Grills on Wheels

Student Project

Grills on Wheels was our first major project using APIs and JQuery. Grills on Wheels was an app designed to connect local Austin food trucks with the food lovers around town. It uses Twitter and Google maps APIs to generate a location for each food truck and their Twitter account for menu updates or location changes. As a group we decided to use a simple color scheme inspired by Austin Street art, which led the design of the icons based around local Austin sites. I was in charge of the front end development and took special consideration into the sites mobile accessibility knowing users will want to access the site on the go.
