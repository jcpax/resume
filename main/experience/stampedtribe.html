Stamped Tribe Website

I worked closely with the Stamped Tribe team to create their website to fit their growing brand. With my expertise in design and development, I was able to listen to their needs for the site and help create the vision to achieve those goals. A heavy emphasis came on images because of the nature of a travel site; therefore, I utilized carousels and image galleries to highlight locations where members had traveled to.

Stamped Tribe Logo

Stamped Tribe is a professional open source community that connects travelers with itineraries and travel ideas. Their logo needed to stand out and bring a contemporary look to travel planning. I created the logo by combining the look of a passport stamp and interjected a modern minimalist icon design. The departing jet gives the logo movement, as well as, breaks the mold of traditional travel staples.
